Mission Statement

Rooted, Poured & Planted Co. creates meaningful products and experiences that restore dignity, plant hope, and build lasting community — with every purchase directly supporting KIICC4Hope and its mission of restoration, housing, and healing.

We believe that the restoration of community starts at home — that’s why home is at the heart of everything we do. We proudly provide jobs and opportunities for individuals in our local community — especially those who may face barriers to traditional employment. Every product and service purchased helps create safe, purpose-driven work for people rebuilding their lives with dignity and hope.

💝 Your Purchase = Purpose

Every gift purchased, service booked, or product enjoyed through Rooted: Poured & Planted Co. becomes a tangible expression of love in action. This is more than a catalog — it’s a movement of mission-aligned generosity.

  • 🛠️ Jobs with Dignity: Each order helps provide safe, supported employment for individuals rebuilding after hardship.
  • 🎁 Mission in Every Package: Whether it’s a comfort jar or a party rental, each offering includes a message of hope and an impact touchpoint.

By choosing us, you’re not just buying — you’re building.
